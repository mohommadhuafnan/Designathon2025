About Designathon

Designathon is a 24-hour creative competition organized by the Society of ICT (SICT). It aims to build confidence and encourage students to take part in national and university-level competitions.

Join us to learn, practice, and gain the experience you need to compete with confidence.

Rules & Guidelines

Team Composition

Each team can have up to 4 members. All members must be students of South Eastern University of Sri Lanka.

Design Tool

All designs must be created exclusively using Figma. Teams must submit a clickable prototype of their solution.

Time Duration

The finale competition will run continuously for 24 hours. Late submissions will not be accepted..

Theme & Problem Statement

The theme or problem statement will be revealed at the start of the event. Designs must align with the given problem.

Originality

All work must be original and created during the event period. Use of pre-made templates is not allowed.

Submission Requirements

Submit your Figma prototype link before the deadline. Include a short description explaining your idea.

Judging Criteria

• Creativity & Innovation
• User Experience (UX)
• Visual Design & Consistency
• Problem Solving Approach
• Presentation Quality

Code of Conduct

• Maintain professionalism and respect throughout
• Organizers' decisions are final
